

Basic App Idea

I have a project. 

The project has many tasks (e.g. run a marathon with many taining runs).

I add my tasks with dates (e.g. 31/1/23 run 5 miles).

I assign a friend to check in with me. 

My friends has a task of checking my task on their list of tasks to check.

My friend has a task and assigns me to check the task. 

I have a task on my tasks to check list.
